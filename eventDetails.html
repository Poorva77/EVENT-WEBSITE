Dataverse Hackathon 2025 - Event Details

  • Team Formation: Form a team of up to 6 members. Each team must include at least one girl member to promote diversity and inclusion.
  • Eligibility: Open to all VIT Bhopal students, regardless of branch or year.
  • Problem Statements: Tackle real-world challenges in Data Science, AI, and Machine Learning, revealed at the event.
  • Mentorship: Get guidance from industry experts and faculty mentors throughout the hackathon.
  • Activities:
    • Icebreaker games and networking sessions
    • Mini coding challenges with instant prizes
    • Project showcase to judges and guests
  • Food & Refreshments: Enjoy complimentary lunch, snacks, and beverages.
  • Prizes & Goodies: Winners receive exciting prizes, certificates, and exclusive club merchandise.
  • Participation Certificates: All participants get digital certificates.
  • Winner Announcement: Results will be declared on September 17, 2025, via the club website and email.
